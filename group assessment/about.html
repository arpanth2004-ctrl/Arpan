About Us

Wanderlust Trails creates simple, helpful travel pages so you can plan trips faster with less stress.

Our Focus

  • Clear destination highlights and routes
  • Hotels & resorts for different budgets
  • Practical tips you can use right away

How We Work

  • One shared stylesheet & script: Every page uses style.css and main.js for a consistent look and lightweight load.
  • Mobile-first layout: The .cards grid auto-fits, and all thumbnails use a uniform 16:9 ratio with object-fit: cover so images look even across pages.
  • Accessible by default: Descriptive alt text, labeled forms, a visible Skip to content link, semantic headings, and readable color contrast.
  • Fast media: Landscape images (~1200×800px, ~150–250KB) with loading="lazy". Video is embedded via YouTube iframe for broad browser support.
  • Simple navigation: Shared header and footer on every page for predictable wayfinding; page highlights use aria-current="page".
  • Light interactivity: A smooth “Back to Top” button on all pages. A welcome alert and one popup are intentionally limited to the home page.
  • Clean HTML: Valid HTML5, responsive viewport, and canonical links help with standards and basic SEO.
  • Source credits: All third-party images/media are listed on References with author, title, year, and link.

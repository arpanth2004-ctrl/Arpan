Career

Love travel and storytelling? Join our team and help us share destinations, tips, and inspiration with the world.

Open Roles

  • Travel Writer — Share stories, guides, and itineraries from global destinations.
  • Photography Contributor — Capture and provide images of landscapes, cities, and culture.
  • Community Coordinator — Connect with readers, manage social media, and grow our travel community.

How to Apply

Email your CV and a short introduction to careers@wanderlusttrails.example. If available, include links to your writing, photography, or social profiles.

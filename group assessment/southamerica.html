South America

From Andean peaks to Amazon rainforests and vibrant cities, South America is rich in nature, history, and culture.

Quick Tips

  • Acclimatize gradually when visiting high-altitude cities.
  • Use long-distance buses or regional flights to cover big distances.
  • Stay aware of safety tips in busy urban areas.
  • Check weather conditions — they vary by region and altitude.

Travel Tips

Practical Tips

  • Keep a copy of your passport and important documents.
  • Buy travel insurance that covers both health and belongings.
  • Use a local SIM or eSIM for internet and maps.

Budget Hacks

  • Use overnight buses or trains to save on accommodation.
  • Cook meals in hostel kitchens or buy local street food.
  • Check if cities offer day passes for attractions and transport.
